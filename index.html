COMPANY OVERVIEW

Miter Derivatives operating as Miter is an emerging Real Estate services/solutions company offering services such as; Real Estate Advisory, Property Development, Property Acquisitions & Sales, Property lettings & leasing, Property Portfolio Management, Facility Management, Project Management, Short-let Apartments and Real Estate Investment packages.

Miter caters to establishments and individuals, with a collective team experience spanning over a decade across Business Advisory & Development, Marketing & Sales, core Real Estate services, Project Management, Construction.

Our Vision

To become a leading, value driven Property Services Company with a knack for innovation powered and consumer centered solutions/services, optimizing multiple facets of the Real Estate value chain across Sub-Saharan Africa.

Our Mission

Miter aims to consistently deliver apt, easily accessible, timely and professional Real Estate services/solutions to ensure customer satisfaction and delivery-on-promise for every assignment/engagement.
